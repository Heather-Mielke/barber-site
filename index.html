Welcome to Barber|sss

Barber|sss was established in 2022 by three men with a combined 25 years experience (Shawn, Steven, Sam). Shawn has been a barber for 10 years, Steven for 9 years, and Sam for 6 years. Barber|sss is an all inclusive barbershop that focuses on making you look your best for any occasion. We provide an array of services such as haircuts, facials and waxing.
